What's this?

This custom interface offers a streamlined, minimalist design tailored for Blackthorn, a Classic+ Dark Age of Camelot freeshard project.

It reduces visual clutter so you can focus on your adventures. Whether in RvR combat or PvE quests, essential information is always available at a glance. Paired with a custom ReShade preset to realy make the game pop!

Features

No bars, no problem!

At 100% value, status bars are transparent. As soon as you need them, they show up!

Cleans up the screen when nothing is happening, for a clutter free experience.

Hidden buttons!

Click your target bar to /face
and right edge to /stick

Click your hp bar with a pet active to target it

Click your power or endurace bar to toggle /sit

Group windows!

10 alternatives to choose from. Small, big, long, grids..

Have it whatever way you want, find the right fit for your class.
All in-game, no need to relog!

Installation Guide

Getting started is easy. Follow these steps:

  1. Download the .7z file: Latest Release
    Optional: If you already have a "custom" folder, rename the "custom" folder in your "daocblackthorn\ui" to "custom-old"

  2. Extract the contents from the bahui.7z file to your "daocblackthorn\ui" folder.
    Overwrite any files when asked, backups are included.

  3. Install ReShade : pick directx 9, when asked if you want to load an ini file, select the "BahShade.ini" file in your "daocblackthorn\ui"
    This will install the shaders needed, and nothing else. Should run smooth on any* system.
